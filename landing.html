FAQ

Common Questions

Is TradeROW really free?
Yes — completely free, forever. No subscription, no credit card, no free trial. Every feature including the leaderboard, AI smart search, analytics, and cloud sync is included at no cost.
What is the Tilt Meter and can I turn it off?
The Tilt Meter is a real-time emotional state tracker that monitors your trading behavior — revenge trades, risk violations, over-trading — and builds a tilt score. At 100% it locks your journal to force a cooldown. It resets every morning in your timezone. You can choose to ignore the warnings, but the lock enforces your own rules. That's the point.
How does the Discipline Lock quiz work?
When your journal locks from high tilt, clicking "Unlock Journal" presents a randomized 4-question multiple choice quiz drawn from a 7-question bank covering stop-losses, risk management, revenge trading, and your trading plan. All 4 must be answered correctly. Wrong answers re-shuffle the questions. This ensures you've genuinely reflected before re-entering the market.
How does the AI Smart Search work?
Every trade you log gets converted into a semantic vector by Pinecone AI and stored in a vector database. When you search, your query is also vectorized and matched against all your trades by meaning — not just keywords. This means searching "trades where I was emotional" actually finds those trades even if you wrote "got caught up in the move and entered too late."
How is rank earned?
Rank is earned through discipline score — not P&L. You gain rank points by following your plan, passing your risk guardrails, completing quests, and maintaining low tilt. You lose rank from violations, tilt locks, and breaking your own rules. There are 21 tiers total from G-LOW to S-TOP.
Is my trading data private?
Yes. Your trade data is stored in your own Supabase cloud instance and never shared with anyone. The leaderboard is opt-in only — your stats only appear publicly if you explicitly choose to join it. You can opt out at any time.
What markets does TradeROW support?
All of them. TradeROW automatically detects Forex, Crypto, Stocks, Futures, Options, Bonds, Commodities, and Indices from the pair name you enter. There's no manual category selection needed.
